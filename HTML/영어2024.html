2024 ESG Sustainability Report

ESG Management Goals

In 2023, NewOne established an ESG strategic framework focused on an ESG action plan that aligns with the company's mission and enables tangible results in sustainable management. In 2024, this framework has been further enhanced as a strategy to achieve sustainable growth and satisfy both internal and external customers.

ESG Implementation Framework

ESG Implementation Schedule
ESG Materiality Assessment

For the action items identified through ESG activity reviews, priorities were set based on urgency, importance, and sequence, following discussions with management.

Importance was assessed based on the financial impact of each indicator, while urgency was evaluated based on the level of time and resources required to establish management systems and implement improvement activities. Based on this, priority tasks were selected to ensure effective ESG activities.

ESG Strategic Tasks and Performance Indicators

Based on the priority of tasks, eight ESG strategic tasks were selected, and performance indicators (KPIs) were set for each task to achieve them. These strategic tasks and performance indicators serve as a roadmap for achieving ESG goals and play a key role in strengthening NewOne's sustainability and responsible management.

Environment

Establishing the Foundation for Environmental Management

Reduction of Greenhouse Gas Emissions

Newon is making multifaceted efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions generated during daily business operations. In particular, the company is striving to decrease both direct emissions from logistics vehicles and indirect emissions resulting from electricity use, with all employees participating in the achievement of these environmental goals. Newon aims to strengthen its greenhouse gas reduction activities and foster a responsible corporate culture for a sustainable future.

Logistics Vehicles

To reduce greenhouse gas emissions and promote eco-friendly management, Newon is gradually replacing its logistics fleet with high-efficiency and eco-friendly vehicles. The company has transitioned corporate vehicles to electric models, replaced old vehicles with fuel-efficient ones, and leased hybrid cars for business use to enhance energy efficiency. These efforts have significantly reduced carbon emissions during transportation and maximized energy savings.

In addition, Newon improves fuel efficiency and reduces greenhouse gas emissions through safe driving education and consistent-speed driving practices. Regular vehicle maintenance, promotion of public transportation, and encouragement of carpooling further minimize energy consumption during logistics operations, contributing to the company’s commitment to a sustainable environment.

Newon actively promotes strategies that enhance resource efficiency and reduce greenhouse gas emissions through cost savings. Employees make environmentally conscious decisions in their daily tasks and perform their duties in sustainable and eco-friendly ways, fulfilling their environmental responsibilities. Through this approach, Newon aims to create a positive environmental impact for its stakeholders and position itself as a leading company driving change toward a more sustainable future.

Electricity

Newon is taking various measures to reduce electricity consumption and improve energy efficiency. The company has introduced high-efficiency appliances and replaced all lighting with LEDs, significantly reducing energy use related to lighting. These initiatives have contributed to lowering power costs and minimizing environmental impact.

Additionally, Newon practices practical energy-saving habits, such as turning off lights and shutting down office and warehouse equipment after working hours.

With the planned relocation of its factory in 2025, the company is also reviewing the introduction of solar panels and natural lighting systems to further promote the use of renewable energy and energy-saving initiatives. These plans not only enhance the facility’s energy efficiency but also play a vital role in achieving Newon’s eco-friendly management goals.

Through these initiatives, Newon has exceeded its greenhouse gas intensity reduction target (a 5% reduction compared to the previous year). Notably, the logistics vehicle sector achieved a 30% improvement year-on-year, while electricity consumption improved by 18%. The company will continue to pursue ongoing improvements and innovations to further reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Employees at Newon are voluntarily engaging in various activities to achieve the company’s greenhouse gas reduction targets. The organization is also fostering an energy-saving culture by continuously exploring process improvements and innovative implementation strategies.

Since declaring its ESG Management in April 2023, Newon has taken responsibility for building a sustainable future by establishing and actively implementing its own environmental regulations. The company has strengthened its waste management systems and built a recycling process to reduce waste generated during distribution, promoting resource circulation. Through these sustainable initiatives, Newon strives to build trust with stakeholders and create long-term positive impacts.

Waste Reduction and Resource Recycling

Through the Logistics Team’s quality circle activities in 2023, Newon has continuously implemented the “Packaging Material Reduction Plan,” taking the lead in environmental protection. By categorizing ordered items by type and applying optimal box placement, the company minimizes packaging material use, thereby contributing to resource conservation and waste reduction.

Newon is also taking various measures to reduce electricity consumption and improve energy efficiency. The company has introduced high-efficiency appliances and replaced all lighting with LEDs, significantly reducing energy use associated with lighting. These efforts have contributed to lowering electricity costs and minimizing environmental impact.

Recycling Service for Surplus Materials

Based on the results of the 2024 customer experience survey, Newon launched the “Material Recycling Service (MRS)” project to recycle unused or surplus electrical, communication, and fire protection materials that are often discarded or left unused at construction sites. Through the establishment of an online B2B surplus material sharing platform connecting suppliers and consumers, the company aims to promote resource circulation as well as sustainable consumption and production.

In particular, the surplus material recycling platform allows users to directly register surplus items left at construction sites, or to exchange or sell materials with other users who need them. This platform plays a key role in reducing industrial waste and promoting resource circulation, thereby contributing to environmental protection.

Establishing a Foundation for Environmental Equipment Manufacturing

Through its ESG management practices, Newon recognizes the importance of preventing serious industrial accidents for sustainable operations. The company has initiated a business in environmental detection and exhaust equipment manufacturing to help prevent occupational hazards caused by pollutants that may occur in automated production environments of large and mid-sized corporations. By creating a safe and stable manufacturing environment, Newon contributes to continuous production and the establishment of a foundation for corporate profitability. Furthermore, client companies adopting Newon’s equipment can also expand their own ESG management initiatives.

Since October 2023, Newon has been developing and manufacturing environmental detection and exhaust systems designed to monitor, measure, and treat hazardous pollutants that may occur during semiconductor manufacturing processes, thereby protecting both workers and the environment. Working in collaboration with external partners for manufacturing and software programming, Newon exclusively supplies these systems to global semiconductor companies—earning recognition for its quality, technology, and commitment to becoming an eco-friendly company that values both people and the environment.

To evolve into a leading manufacturer of environmental equipment, Newon has established an ambitious facility investment plan and is steadily executing it. The company plans to install inspection and testing equipment—currently used for outsourced products—by June 2025 (10 units) and December 2025 (6 units) to ensure process stability. Following this, Newon intends to introduce mass production facilities for in-house manufacturing, aiming to internalize the entire production process.

The environmental equipment production process consists of a total of 112 steps, with only fully tested and approved products proceeding to packaging and shipment. Newon is also striving to minimize defects through rigorous testing and strict quality inspections at every stage of the process.

Environmental Technology Patent Applications

Following its 2018 certification as a MAIN Biz (Management Innovation SME) and 2023 ISO45001 (Occupational Health and Safety Management System) certification, Newon obtained ISO9001 (Quality Management System) and INNO Biz (Technology Innovation SME) certifications in 2024. To promote its environmental equipment manufacturing business for building environmental facility infrastructure, the company has filed multiple patents related to environmental technologies (2023–2024). Among these, three patents have been registered and two are under review. Through continuous research and technological development, Newon will focus on strengthening and maintaining its technological capabilities in the battery and environmental equipment sectors.

In addition, Newon is preparing to transition its business from comprehensive construction material distribution to electronic sensing equipment manufacturing. As the first step in this transition, a research and development department was established within the company in August 2023. Newon continues to invest steadily in infrastructure by expanding research personnel and facilities, with plans to develop the department into an officially certified corporate research institute.

Certification of the Corporate Research Institute scheduled for 2025.

The patent system is designed to promote national industrial development by protecting and encouraging inventions of specific technologies. To be granted, a patent must satisfy three core requirements—novelty, inventiveness, and industrial applicability—and pass a rigorous substantive examination. To date, Newon has filed a total of five patents, of which three have successfully met all requirements and been registered. In return for disclosing its technology through patent applications, Newon has been granted patent rights and design registrations. (Two applications remain under review.)

Acquisition of Inno-Biz (Technology Innovation SME) Certification

Under the mission “To become a company trusted by everyone,” Newon has been expanding various environmental activities and business initiatives since its 2023 ESG Management Declaration. Alongside the establishment of “Newon Vision 2028” in 2024, the company has accelerated efforts in eco-friendly management—the first pillar of ESG management. As part of these efforts, Newon continues to advance its business model integrating both operations and environmental sustainability through the establishment of environmental facility infrastructure, offline logistics service systems, online material distribution services (MRS), and the development and production of environmental devices.

Furthermore, after acquiring the MAIN Biz (Management Innovation SME) certification in 2018 and ISO 45001 (Occupational Health and Safety Management System) certification in 2023, Newon obtained ISO 9001 (Quality Management System) certification in 2024. By building an online platform for material distribution, advancing smart services (automating shipment operations), developing surplus material storage and recycling services for construction sites, and creating pollution detection devices for production environments, Newon earned the Inno-Biz (Technology Innovation SME) certification in November 2024. This recognition officially establishes Newon as a company equipped with excellence in quality, safety, management, and technology innovation.

Establishing a Manufacturing Base through Business Transformation

Newon is preparing to transform its current business from electrical machinery and equipment wholesale (Industry Code 46595) into electronic sensing equipment manufacturing (Industry Code 26294), which includes harmful gas measurement devices, filtration systems, malfunction detection instruments, and smart medical robots. To support this transition, the company opened a manufacturing plant at 53 Jeonpa-ro, Manan-gu, Anyang in May 2024, where products manufactured by outsourcing partners are packaged and shipped. Additionally, Newon plans to upgrade its existing R&D department into a certified corporate research institute by 2025 and internalize its manufacturing technologies alongside the new factory facilities scheduled for completion in May 2025. Through this effort, the company aims to achieve sustainable regional growth, enhance corporate value, and contribute to the community as a leading environmental equipment manufacturer.

Enhancing Eco-Friendly Corporate Image through Green ABS (Asset-Backed Securities) Issuance

In 2024, Newon was selected as an eligible participant in the Ministry of Environment’s Green Asset-Backed Securities (ABS) issuance support program. Through this initiative, the company has been raising business funds for eco-friendly manufacturing, as well as producing and supplying related products. As a result, Newon has further strengthened its image as an environmentally responsible company.

Through this project, Newon received a suitability rating from external institutions regarding environmental criteria such as the Green Taxonomy. By implementing such eco-friendly initiatives, the company strives not only for corporate growth but also to fulfill its social responsibilities as a sustainable enterprise.

Securing Growth Momentum through the Establishment of a New Factory Site

In 2024, Newon secured a new logistics warehouse and advanced factory site in the Baeyang-dong area of Hwaseong City, laying the foundation for its leap forward as a strong and innovative SME through the construction of a new headquarters and factory. The new facility, planned on a 1,800-pyeong (approximately 5,950 m²) site, will feature an integrated O4O (Online for Offline) total distribution system, a surplus material recycling platform (MRS: Material Recycling System), and smart business and environmental infrastructure such as hazardous material detection and exhaust systems. In addition, various eco-friendly facilities—including solar panels, LED lighting, and EV charging stations—will be introduced to promote energy efficiency and greenhouse gas reduction. The entire facility will be operated as an environmentally friendly system that prioritizes quality, safety, and sustainability. Furthermore, employee welfare and well-being will be enhanced through on-site amenities such as a fitness center.

Through the establishment of its new logistics warehouse and factory, Newon aims to diversify its operations from offline-centered distribution to an O4O distribution model, transforming from a general distribution business into a manufacturing and eco-friendly platform enterprise that produces sustainable products and provides surplus material recycling services. Through these efforts, Newon will accelerate the achievement of its “Vision 2028” goals and continue striving for sustainable growth.

Newon is committed to building a healthy and safe work environment by adhering to industrial safety regulations and strengthening health-related initiatives. Creating a Safe Workplace through Zero Industrial Accidents

To prevent serious accidents and enhance proactive safety management, Newon has established an Occupational Safety and Health Management System. Accident Prevention and Medical Support

To prevent workplace accidents, Newon ensures compliance with occupational safety and health standards. Beginning in 2024, health checkup benefits were expanded to include employees’ family members as part of the company’s proactive approach to health and safety management.

Newon has provided financial support for 10 medical cases related to employee health, prevention, and treatment. The company will continue to expand these support measures, prioritizing the health and safety of all employees.

Product Safety and Quality Enhancement

To improve customer satisfaction and enhance external credibility, Newone has established and implemented a detailed management plan to reduce the purchase of defective products, order errors, and defective shipments, thereby eliminating customer inconvenience such as returns.

Quality Enhancement through Process Optimization (Purchase to Shipment)

In 2023, Newone reorganized its purchase-to-shipment process to improve issues related to defective purchases, shipment errors, and resulting returns or disposals. Based on this, each process step has been clearly and specifically defined by department to ensure thorough quality enhancement.

Implementation of WMS (Warehouse Management System)

To further systematize the process from purchase to shipment, Newone continued its efforts and was selected for the 2023 Smart Advancement Support Project, leading to the introduction of a WMS (Warehouse Management System), which was completed in June 2024.

Systematic Return Process and Return Data Management

Newone categorizes return reasons into detailed classifications such as sales request, input error, purchase mistake, and supplier defect, managing each cause accordingly. When a return occurs, the sales team verifies the cause and records it on the document before proceeding, enabling systematic management of return data.

Quality Management System Certification

In August 2024, Newone acquired ISO 9001 Quality Management Certification to provide products that meet customer needs and the highest quality standards. The quality management system is an enterprise-wide management approach led by top management, in which all members participate in every aspect of business activities — from planning and design to purchasing, production, delivery, and service — to meet customer requirements, ensure satisfaction, and maintain quality. Through this system, Newone strives to provide the best products and services to its customers.

Smart Factory Consulting

Since 2023, Newone has been expanding into the field of manufacturing environmental equipment required in semiconductor production. To enhance efficiency, productivity, and customer satisfaction across all stages — from design and development to manufacturing, logistics, and distribution — Newone is working to establish a smart factory that integrates information and communication technology (ICT). Through digital transformation (DX) consulting, the company has analyzed its current smart factory maturity level and is reinforcing infrastructure in stages based on the diagnostic results.

Diagnostic results showed that Newone’s smart factory level — previously focused solely on distribution — was at Level 1 (the initial stage on a five-level scale). Leadership, strategy, and logistics operations were relatively strong, whereas product development, production planning, process control, quality management, information systems, equipment automation, and performance areas were weaker.

Based on the analysis, Newone is expanding the scope of its current ERP system in the information systems domain and promoting the introduction of MES and SCM expansion, as well as the enhancement of data infrastructure for proactive management of customer complaint risks. Through continuous advancement across these areas, the company aims to achieve Level 3 smart factory maturity in the long term.

Creating a Great Workplace through Organizational Culture Assessment

To embody its management philosophy of becoming a trusted company and a great place to work, Newone is assessing its current organizational culture and laying the foundation for a healthy culture that matches its external growth.

Internal Satisfaction Survey

To identify satisfaction and dissatisfaction factors among employees and develop effective solutions for organizational revitalization, Newone conducted a satisfaction survey across seven categories and twenty-one items covering overall HR systems and company operations.

Survey Results

The overall organizational culture satisfaction score was 81.0, slightly above the average level (80). Company satisfaction (83.4) and communication (83.1) scored higher, while HR systems (78.1) and knowledge management (79.0) were relatively lower. Overall, employee satisfaction with the company was favorable, but there is a need to strengthen HR development to support self-improvement and career growth through better working conditions and training programs.

Analysis

Employees expressed satisfaction with the organization’s culture of shared growth and collaboration. However, they also desire more practical communication, clearer role distribution, and a structured job framework. Providing defined career paths and diverse growth opportunities will foster mutual development between individuals and the organization — an essential foundation for sustainable growth.

Organizational Redesign

1. Design Principle: To successfully achieve the company’s vision, it is essential to clarify unclear roles and responsibilities, and reorganize into a core function-centered structure that maximizes organizational performance.

2. Improved Organization: The new structure focuses on functional efficiency by clearly defining “responsibility and authority” to enhance productivity. The design emphasizes consistency between tasks and the organization.

Promoting Sustainable Management through Digital Transformation (DX)
(Vision 2028: KRW 100 Billion in Sales, KOSDAQ Listing, Gyeonggi-do Small Giants)

In the era of the Fourth Industrial Revolution, digital transformation (DX) is not an option but an essential management innovation for strengthening corporate competitiveness and achieving sustainable growth. Under its strategic goal to achieve “Vision 2028,” Newon recognized the need for DX advancement in both manufacturing and marketing sectors and, with support from the Gyeonggi-do Economic Science Promotion Agency, established DX-based growth strategies and implementation plans. The company continues to accelerate digital innovation through structured execution and phased objectives.

Background of DX Implementation

Newon initiated DX to establish a foundation for continuous growth through digital innovation, enhance ERP utilization, and advance its organizational operation system with detailed action plans across core areas.

Purpose of DX Implementation

Establishment of a DX-based production information management roadmap

Manufacturing DX: Develop improvement priorities and smartization roadmaps for P, Q, C, and D areas, along with DX implementation measures

Marketing DX: Develop execution strategies for mid- to long-term platform business expansion based on BM analysis

DX Implementation Strategy

Through digital transformation, Newon is reshaping work processes and establishing long-term business models. Annual DX tasks were defined to materialize these strategies, ensuring a structured and efficient approach. For successful DX execution, Newon established mid- to long-term goals and plans for business models, customer experience, operational processes, and DX implementation systems. This approach enables the company to continuously strengthen its digital capabilities and enhance competitiveness through phased and actionable initiatives.

Through comprehensive analysis—including stakeholder interviews and process assessments— Newon derived five key DX strategies: ① Establishment of a data-driven management foundation, ② Development of an online platform system, ③ Digitalization and advancement of processes, ④ Activation of a digital organizational culture, and ⑤ Establishment of a DX execution framework. These initiatives aim to systematically promote digital transformation and enhance corporate competitiveness.

Marketing DX

Based on SWOT analysis, Newon identified three major Marketing DX improvement tasks: entering the online surplus materials distribution platform business, building a customer portal system, and strengthening marketing DX infrastructure and operational systems. These efforts aim to enhance customer experience, streamline marketing operations, and strengthen digital competitiveness.

The Surplus Material Recycling Platform supports the collection, storage, and utilization of unused electrical, communication, and water-related materials from construction sites. This system promotes environmental sustainability, economic efficiency, and the discovery of new customers.

To build marketing and sales infrastructure, Newon plans to establish a customer database and management system, strengthen marketing/sales teams and structures, and enhance productivity through the use of generative AI.

Manufacturing DX

Through smart factory level assessments, value chain analysis, and business model analysis, Newon identified key innovation areas for digital transformation and established its mid- to long-term DX vision. To realize this vision, the company set strategic objectives and four main focus areas, implementing structured and sustainable DX execution plans.

With a vision of becoming a global SME specializing in AI-based environmental equipment components, Newon pursues technological infrastructure innovation, marketing innovation for new customer acquisition, and production/logistics process innovation. The company is dedicating its full capabilities to sustainable growth and global competitiveness through “ESG-based digital transformation innovation.”

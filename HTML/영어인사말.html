We are Newone a company

COO Cheongo Cho

Since its establishment in 2005 as a supplier of electrical construction materials, Newone. has grown into a specialized distribution company centered on the distribution of electrical, communication, and firefighting equipment. With 39 employees, 392 suppliers, and 414 partner clients, Newone achieved a meaningful milestone in 2024 with annual sales of 25.1 billion KRW. In particular, with the full-scale expansion into environmental equipment manufacturing, we have strengthened our position as an eco-friendly technology-based manufacturer by supplying products to major clients such as Samsung Electronics and SK hynix. Furthermore, we have solidified our foundation for sustainable growth through achievements such as Main-Biz Certification, selection as a Green ABS (Asset-Backed Securities) issuer, Venture Business Certification, Inno-Biz Certification, continued maintenance of ISO 9001 and ISO 45001, continuous patent registrations, and the construction of a new factory (scheduled).

Since 2022, we have been actively implementing ESG management. Following the publication of our first Sustainability Report in 2023, we focused on embedding ESG strategies and enhancing execution capabilities in 2024. This effort went beyond institutional adoption—it was a process of redefining our purpose and direction as a company. Through ESG management, Newone practices the philosophy: “If you want to go far, go together.” We believe the source of sustainable growth lies in people, technology, and trust. Our goal is to build a company that can operate autonomously even in the absence of its CEO—a workplace where employees find joy in their work, and a company that shares value with customers, partners, and the community. Based on transparent and stable management, continuous technological innovation, and a deep sense of responsibility toward the environment and society, Newone will continue to move forward as a sustainable company where everyone grows together.

Together with you, Newone. will continue to grow further and deeper. Thank you.

CEO Yulbeom Lee
